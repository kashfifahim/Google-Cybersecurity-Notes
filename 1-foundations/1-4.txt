SIEM tool is an application that collects and analyzes log data to monitor critical activities in an organization 
A log is a record of events that occur within an organization's systems 
SIEM tools reduce the amount of data an analyst must review by providing alerts for specific types of threats, risks, and vulnerabilities

network protocol analyzers (packet sniffers)
a tool designed to capture and analyze data traffic in a network 
the tool keeps a record of all the data that a computer within an organization's network encounters 

a playbook is a manual that provides details about any operational action 
organizations usually have multiple playbooks documenting processes and procedures for their teams to follow 

chain of custody playbook is the process of documenting evidence possession and control during an incident lifecycle 
protecting and preserving evidence playbook is the process of properly working with fragile and volatile digital evidence 
order of volatility is a sequence outlining the order of data that must be preserved from first to last 
volatile data is data that may be lost if the device in question powers off regardless of the reason
